City Climate Analysis

Similarities: They're all on the coast so they all deal with winds and ocean currents. Most are around the same distance from the equator.

Differences: East coast cities like Miami and Tokyo have warm ocean currents, so they're hot and humid. West coast cities like Lima and Seattle have cold currents, so they're cooler and drier. It makes a huge difference.

Greater Impact: Ocean currents matter more than winds for these cities. Water stays hot or cold way longer than air, so whatever temperature the ocean is really affects the land nearby. That's why Lima is basically a desert even though it's right on the coast.
